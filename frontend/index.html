How it works

Intelligent Document Intelligence

From upload to insight in seconds. Our RAG pipeline ensures every answer is grounded in your actual data.

📤

Upload Any File

Drag & drop or browse to upload PDFs, Word docs, spreadsheets, presentations, and more. We handle the parsing.

🔍

Vector Search

Documents are chunked, embedded, and stored in Qdrant vector DB — enabling semantic similarity search across all content.

🧠

LLaMA 3 Answers

Queries are answered using LLaMA 3-70B via Groq, with retrieved context fed directly — no hallucinations, only facts.

⚡

Blazing Fast

Groq's inference engine delivers LLaMA responses at lightning speed. Ask a question, get answers in under a second.

🔒

Context-Grounded

Answers are strictly derived from your uploaded documents. If the answer isn't in your file, the AI will tell you so.

💬

Natural Language

No need for special syntax. Ask questions the same way you'd ask a colleague — the AI understands context and nuance.
